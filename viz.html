The lowest downpayment you can make is 3.5% of the house price. There is an argument for making a big downpayment, like 20%, if you want to make your life easier in the long run. So, the small downpayment calculation is done with 3.5% of the house price and the big downpayment calculation is done with 20%.

You will want to keep your mortgage payments under 28% of your salary (all salaries are pretax), so the mortgage payment and number of years it will take to pay off your mortgage are calculated using this figure.

While this tool allows you to calculate mortgage payments for any salary and house price combination, typically a bank will only let you take out a mortgage that is no more than 3x your salary. So we also tell you if you will be able to get a mortgage at all based on the criteria you've chosen.
